=============Rank 1=============

Rank 1 is probably the easiest rank to grind through.
The first planes you want to get is the P-36C and the P-36G.
The p-36C and G variant is hard hiting, great climb speed and good
turn rate.

The difference with the P-36C and P-36G is the G has two extra .50 cal
which is extremely helpful when facing armor targets like bomber or yak-1.
then spade them.

The next best plane would be the F2A-1 and the F2A-3 buffalo.As you can
probably guess the F2A-3 is better than the A-1 variant it has a great
turn time, great diving speed, and an carrier aircraft. Now it is important
that you spade every aircraft you are good at because once you spade it you become
more powerful.

=============Rank 2=============

Rank 2 gets more fun but also harder and american can be frustrating to fly
but if you uses the right tactics you can the biggest threat in the match. The planes i personally
recommend is the P-400, P-40, and the F4U-A1 corsair..

The P-400 is a good pick for a hard hiting aircraft for rank 2. It has a
20mm cannon mounted is the nose which can destroy an aircraft and almost one shot
and 2 .50 cal also nose mounted and 4 7.62mm wing mounted which is like 4 ak.
Now american can't really dogfight but if you're good you can dogfight
but the best tactic to uses is the boom and zoom tactic. Boom and Zoom is
when you climb and then dive on your enemies, but american aircraft are not the
at climbing so you side climb. an another great tactic is the hit and run.
To do that tactic you have to be fast. Pick your target shoot him then zoom away.

The P-40 warhawk has six .50 cal and its a great turn fighter at low altitude but high altitude
it doesn't do to well. Personally I would uses this aircraft for hit and run so pick your target
careful.

The best plane in rank 2 is the F4U-A1 corsair also call the whispering death and it earn that name.
This aircraft has six 6 .50 cal that destroy any one in its way, it is also a great
turn fighter. Its primary tactic it the boom and zoom and the hit amd run because of its
great speed, acceleration and energy retention and if you play sim battles it will give
you good slive lions.

=============Rank 3=============

"This is where the fun begins". This rank 3 is the best rank to have fun in
and personally the P-63, P-51C-10, and the F4U-D1 corsair are the best at this rank.

The P-63 has one 37mm cannon, 2 .50 cal nose mounted, and 2 .50 wing mounted
which is devastating. You will need to side but the hit and run is awesome
with this aircraft because of it is fire power and it speed. This aircraft
has a great turn time

The P-51C-10 is a different type of Mustang you probably have not seen before.
P-51C-10 has a red tail because of the 332nd Fighter Group which were know as
red tails they were the first African-American fighter group in America.
The P-51C-10 has 4 .50 cal which don't do much, but it highly agile at high speeds
and is great for hit and run. The key to this aircraft is to keep its speed up
and be mindful on who you choose.

The F4U-D1 is a great aircraft for boom and zoom or hit and run.
With 6 .50 cal you are a threat if you know how to use this aircraft.
You will need to side climb and it is not as agile as the A1 variant
but it is very fast so you can hit your enemies and speed away from them
